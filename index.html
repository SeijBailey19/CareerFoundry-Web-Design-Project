Why Did You Choose Computer Science?

I didn't! Computer Science gave me a rude awakening during on the lowest points in my life. I wasn't sure if I wanted to go off to College after High School. While I had done very well my Senior year (3.6), my cumulative GPA was an astounding 2.175. I figured I would do music production somehow somewhere. Then, my college President had paid a visit to my school, saw "AP Comp Science" on my transcript and asked me to major in the field. In return, I would receive a scholarship on the spot for half tuition among a few other things. My class (2018) graduated on June 1. I was in college June 15th. Today, I choose Web Development/Design with a Comp Science degree.

What is Web Development to you?

To me, Web Development is a gateway for me to showcase my creativity. I am a poet, songwriter, music producer, dancer, natural photographer, sketcher and more all rolled into one. If Web Development/Design allows me to take advantage of creativity, then it is definitely a possible candidate for me in the tech field. This is me testing the waters. So far, I haven't drowned, so I would assume I have an idea of what I am capable of.

What Drives You?

There is some raw, sheer, willpower within me that is interested in one thing: Attaining my ideal future. From most people who bring it up, they say I have some unnatural drive about me. Even if I think about giving up, my hands and feet move almost autonomously to get the job done. I always find a way to succeed where I need to. Maybe it's the hunger to prove myself to others? But you should never live your life based on someone else's theory or expectation of you. Maybe, it's to prove the old me wrong; the old me who could not envision something wonderful; the old me who hated trying harder than I needed to; the old me that was fine with "comfortable". Whatever the case, I continue to achieve substantial mental prowess within myself.
